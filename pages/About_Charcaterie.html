About Us

We started our cat cafe business late 2018 and we are based in Surrey BC. Our aim was to create a space where people could come and enjoy some delicious charcuterie boards as well as drinks. We currently partner with local farmers and ranchers to provide some of the ingredients for our boards. So far, so good! We've had a lot of positive feedback from our customers, and we're seeing steady growth every month. As of this year we have been able to release over 50 cats for adoption and they have all found wonderfull forever homes!
